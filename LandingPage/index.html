About WEKEZA

Wekeza is a console application that was developed to help the small chamas among the kenyan Community automate their day to day activities such as depoits and monthly contributions. As a team we saw how the chamas in our communities are becoming more and more and people have issues of records being lost and records being uncorrectly recorded which led to unnecessary problems. We us a team created WEKEZA in order to help face the challange.
